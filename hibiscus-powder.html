About Semparuthi Flower (Hibiscus):-

Hibiscus is a genus of plants with a flower of bright colors. It grows mostly in the tropics, but some species grow in cool climates. Hibiscus often become national/state flowers. The flower usually has five petals. Hibiscus comes in many colors like white to pink, red, purple and yellow.
